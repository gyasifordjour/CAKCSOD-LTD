Our Features

Business Accounts

Streamline your business transactions with our range of business accounts, including checking, savings, and merchant services. Enjoy convenient online banking, mobile deposit, and cash management solutions tailored to your business needs.

Commercial Banking

If you're a corporate client, our dedicated team of relationship managers will provide customized financial solutions to support your business objectives. Benefit from our extensive range of treasury management, trade finance, and cash management services

Business Loans

Access the capital you need to expand your business, purchase equipment, or meet short-term cash flow requirements. Our flexible business loan options come with competitive rates and flexible repayment terms.

About Us

The catholic archdiocese of Kumasi cooperative society for development CAKCSOD was first conceived by the leadership of the church in Kumasi in June 2014 as part of our 10 years grand scheme, strategic plan initiative SPI 2014-2024 . However, it was laid to follow as we partnered with PAK microfinance on 17 September with the opening of a branch at St. Peters cathedral basilica .Bompata
