How CAKCSOD came by:

The catholic archdiocese of Kumasi cooperative society for development CAKCSOD was first conceived by the leadership of the church in Kumasi in June 2014 as part of our 10 years grand scheme, strategic plan initiative SPI 2014-2024 . However, it was laid to follow as we partnered with PAK microfinance on 17 September with the opening of a branch at St. Peters cathedral basilica .Bompata

IMPACT INVESTMENT
Our motivation to start a financial institution such as CAKCSOD was re-ignited to commence by the encouragement of his holiness Pope Francis who is a strong premotor of impact investment for us, impact investment is a way to generate a legimate funds to address social, environment and evangelization concern (cf. Wikipedia)
From 26-28 June 2016, Pope Francis constituted a conference which brought together impact investing expert and Catholic leaders from around the world to explore how the catholic church and other faith-based institutions can harness the power of creative investment to attain and sustain the church social mission (www.crs.org/mediacentre/pop-29june2016)
The conference had theme entitled “MAKING THE YEAR OF MERCY OF YEAR OF IMPACT FOR POOR” which was hosted by the Vatican’s pontifical council for justice and peace and catholic relief services with our first cardinal ,his eminence Peter k. Appiah Turkson playing a leading role .

FIRST BRANCH OF CAKCSOD
Under the impact investment agenda, therefore, CAKCSOD was inaugurated on Sunday 1sth November 2020, the feast of all saints at the St. john of the cross parish at kronum which has vibrant market and a growing commercial Centre nearby the busy Kumasi-Offinso road in the suame municipal in the Ashanti region with the support of the most rev Gabriel Justice Anokye, metropolitan archbishop of Kumasi, a team from the catholic archdiocese of Kumasi pool fund (cakp-fun) board was sent to sunyani diocese on 4th march 2019 to consult with the management team of BACCSOD , which was establish in the 1980s by the most Rev. James Kwadwo

On the 4th may 2019, we invited Mr. Peter Gyarko, the sunyani diocesan general manager to address leaders of our perished and institutions at our bi-annual pastoral council meeting at cathedral social center. His presentation and interaction that ensured affirmed the need to bring into being CAKCSOD

This need was consolidated in a correspondence received from Ashanti regional department of co-operatives (doc/a/fe/v.3/20 dated 19th august 2020. In it, the regional director indicated among other things our cooperative society will be of great benefit to the members therefore. It can help to alleviate the people’s poverty levels and other challenges confronting them in their social endeavors. It will further help in resolving the difficulties people in catchment area face when there is need to access financial credit loans) for micro, small medium size businesses, healthcare, weddings, funerals and education

GOAL AND OBJECTIVE
Hence, our goal is to become a resilient financial institution in the region as we lay the foundation to open cooperative societies in all parishes, rectorates, chaplaincies and school within the catholic archdiocese of Kumasi. In this light, we shall operate by observing pure cooperative principal and practices on a sound moral business lines of supervision, monitoring education and evaluation (SME)
Consequently, the objective of caksod is to create wealth to support the church’s evangelization and her social mission educating and promoting financial literacy and the culture of saving among the people of God

OUR MISSION, VISION AND CORE VALUE
Our Mission
To provide a reliable, convenient and efficient co-operative service in finance with the aim of addressing poverty and developmental needs

Our Vision
To alleviate poverty among members of the co-operative society

Our core value
